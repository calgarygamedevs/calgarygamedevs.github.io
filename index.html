The Calgary Game Developers Association is an open and welcoming community of people involved with creating video games in the Calgary area. Our goal is to foster local video game creation and encourage those interested in making games to actively participate within the community.

Our group is extremely active, with multiple events happening each week. Check our Meetup.com page for a list of our upcoming gatherings.
