The Calgary Game Developers Association is a not-for-profit organization dedicated to building a local community around creating video games. Regardless of skill level or profession, we encourage anyone interested in making games to come out to meetups, meet other members, and build up your passion towards making awesome games!

We host a variety of meetups and events which are listed below. If you’re in the Calgary area we encourage you to check out our meetup page and RSVP to an event, and we hope to see you out!

Events

Weekly

  • Moonlight Tuesday
  • Indie Friday

Location: The Inc.

Let's work together! We host weekly coworking sessions with support from Innovate Calgary. Every Tuesday and Friday members of our community gather to work on games in a more social environment. Please note this is a work session and not ideal for socializing.

Price: $20/month

* Membership required

Monthly

  • Social Meetup

Location: Rose & Crown Pub

Held on the third Thursday of every month, this meetup is great for networking, casual conversation, and showing what you’re working on! We also have a community presentation on a select game development topic. If you're new to the group, this meetup the best place to start!

Tri-annual

  • Game Jams

Location: Varies by event

We host local meetups for the super-popular online game jam Ludum Dare! Come out on the Friday to hash out ideas with other members and form teams, then use the weekend to make your ideas playable!

Annually

  • Indie Game Bash

Location: Varies by event

Serving as our annual fundraiser, the Indie Game Bash is a celebration of couch multiplayer video games! Come out to experience the social side of indie games from all over the world, as well as games made by local Calgary developers. Grab a ticket and come party with us! (18+ Only)

More information: indiegamebash.com

Membership

Our monthly social and critique will always be free for anyone to attend. We also offer a membership with the following benefits:

  • Primary placement for public showcases
  • One free work session at Innovate Calgary to get a feel for the space
  • A vote during our AGMs

Price: $30/annually
